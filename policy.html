Privacy Policy

At Sukkshma Bio LLP, protecting your privacy is of utmost importance. This Privacy Policy details how we handle, store, and protect your information while ensuring compliance with data protection regulations.

1.1 We may collect data related to your interactions with our research documents, inquiries about products, and technical requests.

1.2 Details such as your name, email, organization, and professional designation may be collected for communication purposes.

1.3 Cookies and similar technologies may collect details about your browsing behavior to enhance Website performance and ensure secure access.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Internal Operations Your data is used to:

  • Respond to technical inquiries.
  • Provide customized recommendations based on your needs.
  • Improve the functionality of the Website.

2.2 All collected data complies with applicable laws and is handled in accordance with industry standards.

3. Security Measures

3.1 We employ state-of-the-art encryption and secure server technologies to safeguard your information.

3.2 In the event of a data breach, affected users will be notified promptly in compliance with applicable regulations.

4. Rights of Users

4.1 You have the right to:

  • Access and review the data we collect.
  • Request corrections or updates to your personal information.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.

5. Retention Policy

5.1 Data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected or to comply with legal obligations.
