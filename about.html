About Sukkshma Bio LLP

Founded in 2021 in Mumbai, Sukkshma Bio LLP is transforming the biotechnology landscape by delivering globally competitive, indigenously developed products for diagnostics and therapeutics.

The Journey So Far

Our founder envisioned a future where India would lead in biotechnology innovation. With over a decade of R&D expertise, Sukkshma Bio was born to address critical gaps and reduce dependency on international suppliers.
